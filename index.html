Who Is Tetro?

Tetro is a 19 year old male from the United Kingdom, he is a gamer and has many intriguing hobbies and interests, his main hobbies are eSports and coding.

He started his eSports career in a game called Critical Ops, which is a Multiplayer First Person Shooter game, he played with multiple teams as he was improving his skills in the game, below this post is his most popular highlights videos from Critical Ops. Over the years he started to move over to other games such as PUBG Mobile and Fortnite, from this he discovered his love for PUBG which he wanted to persue as a career with a team, unfortunately he didnt have the right equipment to become one of the best, he was playing on an ipad 2017, until the start of 2020 when he decided quit competitive and he wanted to just play for fun with friends, after this he recieved an iPhone 11 as a gift.
